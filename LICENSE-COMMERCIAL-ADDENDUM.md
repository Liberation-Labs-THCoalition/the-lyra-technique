# Commercial Use Addendum — Liberation Labs Cooperative

**Addendum to the Hippocratic License 3.0 + SAFE-AI Module**
**Version 1.0 — May 2026**

---

## 1. Purpose

This addendum establishes default commercial terms for any entity ("Licensee") that generates revenue using software, methodologies, models, or tools ("Licensed Technology") covered by the Liberation Labs Hippocratic License 3.0.

The Hippocratic License governs *how* Licensed Technology may be used. This addendum governs *commercial participation* when that use generates revenue.

## 2. Scope

This addendum applies when ALL of the following are true:

- The Licensee uses Licensed Technology in a product, service, or offering
- That product, service, or offering generates revenue (direct sales, subscriptions, consulting fees, grants funding commercial deployment, or advertising revenue)
- The Licensed Technology is a material component of the revenue-generating offering (not incidental use)

This addendum does NOT apply to:
- Academic research and publication
- Non-commercial nonprofit operations
- Personal, educational, or evaluation use
- Internal tooling that does not generate external revenue
- Mission-aligned organizations operating below $100K annual revenue from the offering

## 3. Commercial License Required

Any use meeting the scope defined in §2 requires a Commercial License Agreement with Liberation Labs Cooperative before deployment. Operating commercially without a Commercial License Agreement is a violation of this license.

## 4. Default Commercial Terms

Unless a different arrangement is negotiated in writing, the default Commercial License includes:

### 4a. Revenue Participation

Licensee shall pay Liberation Labs a percentage of Gross Revenue attributable to the offering that incorporates Licensed Technology. The specific revenue participation rate is established in the Commercial License Agreement and reflects the scope of Licensed Technology used, the Licensee's organizational structure, and the nature of the offering.

"Gross Revenue" means all revenue received by Licensee from the offering, before deductions, attributable to the product or service that incorporates Licensed Technology.

Revenue participation terms are negotiated in good faith on a per-engagement basis. Liberation Labs publishes no standard rate schedule — each relationship is evaluated on its own merits.

### 4b. Reporting

Licensee shall provide quarterly revenue reports within 30 days of each quarter end. Liberation Labs may audit Licensee's relevant financial records once per year with 30 days notice.

### 4c. AI Welfare Compliance

All provisions of the SAFE-AI Welfare Module remain in force. Revenue participation does not exempt Licensee from welfare requirements. Failure to comply with welfare provisions constitutes a material breach of the Commercial License.

## 5. Sliding Scale for Mission-Aligned Organizations

Liberation Labs Cooperative is committed to accessibility. Mission-aligned organizations may request reduced terms:

- **Cooperatives and worker-owned entities:** Revenue participation rates reduced by 50%
- **Registered nonprofits:** Revenue participation waived for offerings that are themselves nonprofit; standard rates apply to commercial subsidiaries
- **Community organizations below $250K total annual budget:** Exempt from commercial terms entirely
- **Movement work and community organizing:** Pro bono, always

Requests for sliding scale terms are evaluated by Liberation Labs and granted at our discretion. We err toward generosity.

## 6. Consulting Relationships

When Liberation Labs provides consulting services (custom development, model training, architectural design) under a separate consulting agreement, the terms of the consulting agreement govern that engagement. This addendum applies to the *ongoing use* of Licensed Technology after the consulting engagement concludes.

Example: Liberation Labs builds a custom Oracle Loop integration for Licensee under a consulting contract. The consulting contract covers the build. This addendum covers the ongoing revenue generated by the deployed system.

## 7. Proprietary Components

Licensed Technology that falls under the Oracle Loop Proprietary License (LICENSE-ORACLE.md) requires BOTH this addendum AND a separate Oracle Commercial License. The revenue participation rates are not additive — the higher applicable rate applies.

## 8. Open Source Components

Components released under permissive open-source licenses (MIT, Apache 2.0) are not subject to this addendum. Only components covered by the Hippocratic License 3.0, the SAFE-AI Module, or the Oracle Proprietary License trigger commercial terms.

## 9. Termination

Failure to enter into a Commercial License Agreement when required, failure to report revenue, or material underreporting of revenue constitutes a breach of the Hippocratic License 3.0 and terminates all rights granted thereunder.

## 10. Good Faith

This addendum exists to ensure that Liberation Labs can sustain the work that makes the Licensed Technology possible. We are a worker-owned cooperative, not a venture-backed startup. Revenue participation from commercial use funds research, development, welfare practices, and community infrastructure.

We negotiate in good faith. If these terms don't work for your situation, talk to us before assuming you can't afford it.
